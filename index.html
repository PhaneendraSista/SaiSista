Artifact 01

Data Engineer Interview Coach

A custom AI assistant I designed during the AI Lab assignment for my AI in Data Analytics course, showing how generative AI can solve a real-world problem: helping aspiring Data Engineers prepare for technical interviews.

Introduction & description

Preparing for Data Engineering interviews means covering a wide range of ground — SQL, Python, Snowflake, AWS, Spark, ETL concepts, and behavioral rounds. Most candidates struggle to organize study material and practice effectively across all of it.

To address that, I built the Data Engineer Interview Coach, a custom GPT that acts as a virtual interview assistant. It reviews resumes, generates personalized study plans, runs mock interviews, explains technical concepts, and gives feedback throughout preparation.

Objective

Design and develop an AI-powered interview assistant that supports Data Engineering candidates with personalized learning resources, technical interview prep, resume reviews, and mock interviews.

Process — a design thinking approach
01 · EMPATHIZE

Understand the problem

Identified common challenges students and professionals face preparing for DE interviews.

02 · DEFINE

Frame the need

Recognized the need for one AI assistant covering technical explanations, coaching, and study plans.

03 · IDEATE

Brainstorm features

Mapped out resume review, SQL/Python practice, Snowflake & AWS guidance, mock interviews, and study planning.

04 · PROTOTYPE

Build

Built a Custom GPT in ChatGPT using structured prompt engineering.

05 · TEST

Refine

Tested with varied prompts, refined responses, and improved the conversational experience iteratively.

Reflection

For my first portfolio artifact, I selected the Data Engineer Interview Coach that I developed during the AI Lab because it best represents my technical skills and demonstrates how Artificial Intelligence can be applied to solve a practical problem. I tailored this artifact for recruiters, hiring managers, and technology professionals by presenting it as a complete AI solution rather than simply describing the assignment. During development, I learned how prompt engineering, iterative testing, and the Design Thinking framework contribute to building user-centered AI applications. I refined the assistant by testing resume reviews, SQL interview questions, and mock interview scenarios to improve the quality and consistency of its responses. This project reflects both my Data Engineering background and my graduate studies in AI in Data Analytics by demonstrating how AI can enhance learning and career preparation. It also represents my commitment to continuous learning and creating practical AI solutions that provide value to end users.
